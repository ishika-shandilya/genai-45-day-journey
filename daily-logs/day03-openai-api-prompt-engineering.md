## 💡 Key insight
Prompt quality = output quality. Same principle as 
defining variables correctly in econometric models.
The AI doesn't get smarter — you get better at asking.

## 😕 Confusion → clarity
Temperature parameter felt abstract at first.
Low temperature = precise and deterministic (good for facts)
High temperature = creative and varied (good for brainstorming)
Think of it like confidence intervals — 
narrow vs wide range of acceptable outputs.
